<MathProblem
  number={7}
  choices={[
    { label: 'A', text: '-7' },
    { label: 'B', text: '7' },
    { label: 'C', text: '-3' },
    { label: 'D', text: '3' }
  ]}
>
  <div slot="prompt">
    <p>Evaluate:</p>
    <p>
      $$
      -\bigl|7 - 3 - 2\bigr| - |-5|
      $$
    </p>
  </div>

  <div slot="hint">
    <p>First simplify inside each absolute value, then take the absolute value, then apply the outside signs. Finally combine the results.</p>
  </div>

  <div slot="solution">
    <p>Inside the first absolute value:</p>
    <p>
      $$
      7 - 3 - 2 = 4 - 2 = 2.
      $$
    </p>
    <p>
      Then:
      $$
      \bigl|7 - 3 - 2\bigr| = |2| = 2,
      $$
      so
      $$
      -\bigl|7 - 3 - 2\bigr| = -2.
      $$
    </p>
    <p>Second absolute value:</p>
    <p>
      $$
      |-5| = 5.
      $$
    </p>
    <p>Combine:</p>
    <p>
      $$
      -\bigl|7 - 3 - 2\bigr| - |-5| = -2 - 5 = -7.
      $$
    </p>
    <p>So the correct choice is <strong>A.</strong> $-7$.</p>
  </div>
</MathProblem>

<MathProblem

  choices={[

>
  <div slot="prompt">
    <p>A bank account starts with a balance of $120$. A withdrawal of $75$ is made. Then a deposit of $40$ is made. Finally, another withdrawal of $25$ is made.</p>
    <p>What is the final balance?</p>
  </div>

  <div slot="hint">
    <p>
      Write each transaction as an integer (withdrawal = negative, deposit = positive) and add them with the
      starting balance:
    </p>
    <p>
      $$
      120 + (\text{change}_1) + (\text{change}_2) + (\text{change}_3).
      $$
    </p>
  </div>

  <div slot="solution">
    <p>Transactions as integers:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Starting balance: $120$</li>
      <li>Withdrawal of $75$: $-75$</li>
      <li>Deposit of $40$: $+40$</li>
      <li>Withdrawal of $25$: $-25$</li>
    </ul>
    <p>Expression:</p>
    <p>
      $$
      120 - 75 + 40 - 25.
      $$
    </p>
    <p>Compute step by step:</p>
    <p>
      $$
      120 - 75 = 45,
      $$
      $$
      45 + 40 = 85,
      $$
      $$
      85 - 25 = 60.
      $$
    </p>
    <p>So the final balance is $60$. The correct choice is <strong>C.</strong> $60$.</p>
  </div>
</MathProblem>
